TREES

A tree is a perennial plant with an
elongated stem,or trunk,supporting
branches and leaves in most species.
The trunk contains woody tissue for
strength,and vascular tissue to carry
materials from one part of the tree to another.
Trees play a significant role in reducing
erosion and moderating the climate.
They remove carbon dioxide from the atmosphere
and store large quantities of carbon in their tissues.
Trees create peaceful,aesthetically
pleasing environment.
They also provide oxygen,improving air quality
and control climate by moderating the
effect of the sun,rain and wind.
